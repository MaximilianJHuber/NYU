Task 6¶

The price of a zero coupon bond with maturity in one year is given by: $$p_{t}(1)=-y_{t}(1)=\underset{A(1)}{\underbrace{-\delta_{0}}}\underset{B(1)}{\underbrace{-\delta_{1}}}X_{t}$$ The Euler equation for holding a bond with a maturity of $n$ years over the next year is: $$p_{t}(n)=log\Big(P_{t}(2)\Big)=log\Big(\mathbb{E}_{t}\Big[M_{t+1}\cdot P_{t+1}(n-1)\Big]\Big)=log\Big(\mathbb{E}_{t}\Big[exp\big(m_{t+1}+p_{t+1}(n-1)\big)\Big]\Big)$$

$$=log\Big(\mathbb{E}_{t}\Big[exp\big(m_{t+1}+A(n-1\big)+B(n-1)^{'}X_{t+1}\Big]\Big)$$ $$=log\Big(\mathbb{E}_{t}\Big[exp\big(-\delta_{0}-\frac{1}{2}\lambda_{t}^{'}\Sigma\lambda_{t}+A(n-1)-\delta_{1}^{'}X_{t}+B(n-1)^{'}\Gamma X_{t}+B(n-1)^{'}\epsilon_{t+1}-\lambda_{t}^{'}\epsilon_{t+1}\big)\Big]\Big)$$ $$=\underset{=: A(n)}{\underbrace{A(n-1)-\delta_{0}+\frac{1}{2}B(n-1)^{'}\Sigma B(n-1)-B(n-1)^{'}\Sigma\lambda_{0}}}\underset{=: B(n)^{'}X_{t}}{\underbrace{-\delta_{1}^{'}X_{t}+B(n-1)^{'}\Gamma X_{t}-B(n-1)^{'}\Sigma\lambda_{1}X_{t}}}$$

Unwinding the recursion for $B(n)$ yields: $$B(n)^{'} = -\delta_1^{'}\sum_{i=1}^{n}\big(\Gamma^*\big)^{i-1}$$
